You are voluntarily engaging in activities that involve:

* **High-Pressure Systems:** Boiling fluids creates pressure vessels. Poorly brazed joints can burst, spraying boiling liquid.
* **High-Speed Projectiles:** 3D-printed turbines spinning at 10,000+ RPM can shatter. Shrapnel can cause blindness or severe injury.
* **High Voltage:** The generator produces unregulated electricity.
